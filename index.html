What will this new framework?

I'm working on making a simple framework EnterIN and unique respecting his "Inception Logic".

Are planned:

  • Quick Configuration and User Friendly
  • Create fast and web pages style "Inception" in HTML5 / CSS3
  • Inception Blog Development compatible with the main CMS for blog including Wordpress
  • Inception Touch / Mobile compatible with iOS , Android , Symbian and Windows Phone
  • Inception Image Effects and 3d without WebGL
  • Inception Events with keyboard and mouse wheel

What is an "Inception Effect" ?

EnterIN has its own logic for page view , and it is the same logic of created plugin for jQuery, that gave birth to the world's EnterIN visits.
The "Inception Effect" is the power of EnterIN!

Why "not WebGL 3D effects" ?

EnterIN wants to enclose all the resources at the time still on the web, but with its innovation.
The Canvas and WebGL would penalize the SEO e and it is for this reason I am working hard to simulate 3D effects with maximum performance!

Why the AGPL 3 license?

EnterIN isn't only a framework: it's a Social Project.
I believe in innovation, development, collaboration, and world sharing, and it's the reason that i have released EnterIN Free and Open Source and chose the AGPL3 license.
Anyone can download, use, and sell it, but the framework can't stop its "cycle" of diffusion and can't therefore be enclosed in a Proprietary Software under another license .
Any changes and any plugin created to EnterIN, it must be released under the same license.
The development and innovation should not be interrupted . This is the philosophy of EnterIN!
